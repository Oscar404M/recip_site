Chocolate Cake


This chocolate cake recipe is a classic .This version of the recipe is fast and quick.



Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 20g

Butter: 200g

sugar : 150g

Flour : 80g

Eggs : 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together.

2

Mix the sugar, and eggs with the Flour.

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
